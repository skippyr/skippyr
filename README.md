## About
A macOS developer dedicated to crafting bittersweet cherry apps for creative people. I consider every project as one of my cherries — each carefully seeded, grown with love, and distributed to the people they are supposed to delight.

I've always been a dreamer with restless ideas. I made mistakes while searching for the place where I belong, and I apologize for them. Everything came together when I founded the Dragon’s Cave orchard, my home where my dreams could finally take root, and I could help others grow theirs too.

I highly appreciate contributions to my projects, especially feedback, issue reports, and pull requests. If you need to talk to me, feel free to [send me an e-mail](mailto:skippyr.developer@icloud.com).
